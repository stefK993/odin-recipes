Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is a typical meal of Italy, pricesly from the northern center regione of Emilia Romagna.

The picture on top is very strange because represent a strange lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato soup
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Take the first lasagna sheet and put it on the plate
  2. Now put the Tomato soup
  3. Now the Mozzarella
  4. At the end the meat
  5. Repeat until you end all the ingredients
  6. Now cook all in the oven
